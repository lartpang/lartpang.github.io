About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in the Faculty of Electronic Information and Electrical Engineering Dalian University of Technology (DUT). I received the B.E. degree in electronic and information engineering from DUT in 2019. My advisors are Prof. Lihe Zhang and Prof. Huchuan Lu from DUT.
I also work concurrently as the Director of Algorithms at X3000 Inspection (pre-A round completed, A round underway), mainly focusing on machine vision based algorithm design and deployment.
My current research interests include Deep Learning, Computer Vision, Multi-Modal/Task Learning, and Neural Network Design. In particular, I focus on RGB/RGB-D/RGB-T Salient Object Detection, RGB/Video Camouflaged Object Detection, RGB/RGB-T Crowd Counting, Remote Sense Change Detection, RGB/RGB-D Image Semantic Segmentation, Unified Architecture Design.
