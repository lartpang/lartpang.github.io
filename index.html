My Experiences

  • Technology Partner & Director of Algorithms at X3000 Inspection (pre-A round completed (2023), A round completed (2024)), focusing on algorithm design and deployment based on the machine vision.
  • Pursuing a Ph.D in Signal and Information Processing and supervised by Prof. Lihe Zhang and Prof. Huchuan Lu (IEEE Fellow) from the School of Information and Communication Engineering, Dalian University of Technology (DUT), 2019 - Present.
  • Received the B.E. degree in Electronic and Information Engineering from the School of Information and Communication Engineering, Dalian University of Technology (DUT), 2015 - 2019.
I work very closely with my best friend, Xiaoqi Zhao, on most of my projects.

Research Interests

My current research interests include Deep Learning, Computer Vision, and Neural Network Design.

In particular, I focus on:
  • Multi-Modal Learning: Vision, Depth, Thermal, Text, Video
  • Open-Vocabulary Segmentation: Open-Vocabulary Camouflaged Object Segmentation
  • Architecture Design: CNN, Transformer, Unified Architecture
  • Human Visual Attention: Salient and Camouflaged Object Perception
  • Context-depend Concept Perception: Salient, Camouflaged, Shadow, Transparent
  • Human-Centered Scene Analysis: Crowd Counting
  • Remote Sense Image Analysis: Remote Sense Change Detection
  • Industrial X-ray/CT Machine Vision: Lithium Battery, FPCA, Auto Parts
  • Medical Image Segmentation: Colon Polyp, COVID-19, Breast, Skin
