Youwei Pang

I am a Ph.D. student in the Faculty of Electronic Information and Electrical Engineering Dalian University of Technology (DUT). I received the B.E. degree in electronic and information engineering from DUT in 2019.

My current research interests include deep learning, computer vision, and image segmentation. In particular, I focus on RGB/RGB-D salient object detection and camouflaged object detection.
