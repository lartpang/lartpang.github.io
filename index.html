Research Interests

My current research interests include Deep Learning, Computer Vision, and Neural Network Design.

In particular, I focus on:
  • Architecture Design: CNN, Transformer, Unified Architecture
  • Human-Centered Scene Analysis: Crowd Counting
  • Remote Sense Image Analysis: Remote Sense Change Detection
  • Human Visual Attention: Salient and Camouflaged Object Perception
  • Context-depend Concept Perception: Salient, Camouflaged, Shadow, Transparent, Abnormal
  • Industrial X-ray/CT Machine Vision (Ai4Industury): Lithium Battery, FPCA, Auto Parts
  • Medical Lesion Segmentation: Colon Polyp, COVID-19, Breast, Skin
  • Learning Paradigm: Multi-Task Learning, Multi-Modal Learning (Vision/Depth/Thermal/Text/Video)
